December Meeting: Year-end Social at Legend Brewing Co.

Details

  • Date: Tuesday, Dec 11
  • Time: 18:00-20:00
  • Place: Legend Brewing Co, 321 West 7th Street, Richmond, VA (map)
  • RSVP: Meetup.com

Social Time!

Come out and have a beer or two with the rest of the group while we reflect on 2012 and dream about 2013.

It's been an eventful year for CVREG but now it's time to relax and prepare for the holidays. To our newest members: come on out and tell us how we can help you learn more about Ruby. To our long-time members: come on out and share your experiences. I know there are a few of us lucky enough to work full time with Ruby; how were you able to do it. Also, come with your ideas for meetings you'd like to see. But most of all: come on out and enjoy the company. See you there!

Remember to bring a friend and spread the word.

Thanks!
