Ruby Two: A Whirlwind Tour

"Ch-ch-Changes
Just gonna have to be a different man
Time may change me
But I can't trace time"
 -- David Bowie, Changes

In this talk, we'll explore the biggest new features introduced by Ruby 2.0, along with some examples of when and where they might be useful. Refinements, kwargs (yay Python!), and other new friends will be joining the Ruby family. We'll also talk about some of the biggest pain points you're likely to experience during an upgrade, and how to mitigate the hurt.

John Feminella

John Feminella is the co-founder of UpHex, a bootstrapped startup trying to shine a light into the darkness of customer analytics for online businesses. You can follow him on Twitter here.
